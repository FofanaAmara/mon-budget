Desktop Design Notes
  • Compact floating widget, bottom-right corner (24px from edges), 360px wide
  • Two states: collapsed pill (340px) and expanded card (360px, max 500px height)
  • Expand animation: scale-in from bottom-right origin (transform-origin: bottom right)
  • Steps in vertical list with timeline connectors (not 2x2 — too narrow)
  • NO backdrop/overlay — widget floats independently, does not dim the page
  • Elevated composite shadow for visual separation from page content
  • Visible on ALL pages until all 4 steps completed
  • Collapse button (chevron down) returns to the compact pill
  • Does not overlap the sidebar (it is in the main content area, right-aligned)
