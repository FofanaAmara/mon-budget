# Brand Identity — Mes Finances
## Direction 2: The Bold Direction

---

## Strategic Foundation

### Archetype
- **Primary: Hero** — "Tu peux y arriver." The Hero transforms through effort and courage. Mes Finances transforms financial fog into mastery through one act of control: setting up your budget.
- **Secondary: Magician** — "Configure une fois, l'app fait le reste." The magic of effortless transformation once the Hero's initial effort is made.

### Brand Personality
**Audacieuse. Directe. Galvanisante.**

This brand doesn't whisper reassurance — it fuels momentum. It treats the user as someone who's READY to take control, not someone who needs hand-holding. The tone is energizing, not anxious. Confident, not aggressive.

### The Core Tension (What Makes It Interesting)
Most finance apps position themselves as either:
- A cold, professional tool (bank energy)
- A gentle, nurturing guide (therapist energy)

Mes Finances in this direction is NEITHER. It's **coach energy** — the person in your corner who believes you can do it, who gives you direct answers, who celebrates your wins. Think less "don't worry, we'll figure it out" and more "ton mois? Sous controle."

### Mission
Donner a chaque personne le pouvoir de maitriser ses finances en 10 secondes par mois.

### Vision
Un monde ou gerer son argent donne de l'energie au lieu d'en drainer.

### Values
1. **Maitrise** — Ton argent repond a TES regles. Pas l'inverse.
2. **Clarte** — Pas de jargon, pas de flou. Une question, une reponse.
3. **Momentum** — Chaque action te rapproche du controle. L'app travaille avec toi.
4. **Confidentialite** — Tes donnees sont les tiennes. Point final.

## Voice

### Personality in 3 Words
1. Directe
2. Motivante
3. Confiante

### How We Speak
- On est **directe**, pas condescendante
- On est **motivante**, pas insistante
- On est **confiante**, pas arrogante
- On tutoie — toujours. "Tu", jamais "vous".

### Tone Attributes
| Attribute | Level (1-10) |
|-----------|-------------|
| Expert | 5 |
| Trustworthy | 8 |
| Warm | 6 |
| Clear | 10 |
| Confident | 9 |
| Playful | 4 |

### Vocabulary
**Mots signature**: Maitrise, Controle, Clarifier, En avant, Ton [argent/budget/mois], Sous controle
**Mots interdits**: Complique, Stressant, Inquiet, Peut-etre, Difficile, On va essayer
**Expressions propres**: "Ton mois, sous controle.", "Prends les devants.", "Budget maitrise."

### Tone by Context
| Context | Adjustment |
|---------|-----------|
| **Marketing** | Maximum energy, motivating, punchy. "Reprends le controle de ton argent." |
| **Product UI** | Clear, direct, confident. "Budget du mois: maitrise." |
| **Support** | Warm but direct. "On regle ca ensemble." |
| **Onboarding** | Encouraging, forward-moving. "3 etapes. Ton budget est pret." |
| **Errors** | Honest, calm. "Quelque chose a bloque. Reessaie." |

### Rhythm
**Staccato.** Short sentences. Punchy. Phrases that land.
Occasional longer sentence for warmth — but the default is impact.

"Ton argent. Ta vision. Tes regles."
Not: "Nous sommes la pour vous aider a mieux gerer vos finances personnelles au quotidien."

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

#### Primary: Coral Vermillion
- **HEX**: #D94F3D
- **RGB**: rgb(217, 79, 61)
- **HSL**: hsl(7, 68%, 55%)
- **Usage**: Brand signature. CTAs, headlines, symbol, key accent. This is the color people remember.
- **Why**: Warm energy without alarm. Distinctive in the finance space (dominated by blues/greens). Says "action" and "warmth" simultaneously. Ownable.

#### Secondary: Midnight Navy
- **HEX**: #1B1F3A
- **RGB**: rgb(27, 31, 58)
- **HSL**: hsl(232, 36%, 17%)
- **Usage**: Dark backgrounds, wordmark on light, body text, grounding element. The trust anchor.
- **Why**: Deep, serious, trustworthy — without being generic corporate blue. The depth and darkness suggest confidence and substance.

#### Accent: Amber Gold
- **HEX**: #E8A435
- **RGB**: rgb(232, 164, 53)
- **HSL**: hsl(37, 79%, 56%)
- **Usage**: Success states, achievements, positive highlights, reward moments. Sparingly — max 10% of any composition.
- **Why**: The Hero's gold — achievement, victory, "you did it." Creates warm triad with coral and navy.

#### Warm White
- **HEX**: #FAF8F5
- **RGB**: rgb(250, 248, 245)
- **Usage**: Primary light background. Warmer than pure white — human, not sterile.

#### Slate (Body Text)
- **HEX**: #3A3D4A
- **RGB**: rgb(58, 61, 74)
- **Usage**: Body text on light backgrounds. High contrast, readable, warm-neutral.

#### Secondary Text
- **HEX**: #8B8FA0
- **RGB**: rgb(139, 143, 160)
- **Usage**: Captions, metadata, secondary information.

#### Border / Divider
- **HEX**: #E8E6E3
- **RGB**: rgb(232, 230, 227)
- **Usage**: Subtle divisions, borders. Warm gray, not cold.

### Accessibility Notes
- Slate (#3A3D4A) on Warm White (#FAF8F5): Contrast ratio ~10.5:1 (WCAG AAA)
- Coral (#D94F3D) on Midnight (#1B1F3A): Contrast ratio ~4.8:1 (WCAG AA for large text)
- Coral (#D94F3D) on Warm White (#FAF8F5): Contrast ratio ~3.6:1 (WCAG AA for large text only — use for headlines, not body)
- Midnight (#1B1F3A) on Warm White (#FAF8F5): Contrast ratio ~14.2:1 (WCAG AAA)

### Typography

#### Display / Headlines: Space Grotesk
- **Weights**: Bold (700), SemiBold (600)
- **Usage**: All headlines, hero text, key numbers, navigation
- **Character**: Geometric with personality. The subtle quirks in letterforms (the tail of the 'a', the angle of the 'e') give it warmth without sacrificing confidence. Modern but not cold.
- **Source**: Google Fonts (free, web-ready)

#### Body / UI: DM Sans
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Usage**: Body text, UI elements, forms, labels
- **Character**: Clean, modern, excellent readability at all sizes. The geometric base matches Space Grotesk, creating a harmonious system. Slightly more neutral, letting headlines carry the personality.
- **Source**: Google Fonts (free, web-ready)

### Logo

#### Concept: "The Summit M"
The symbol is an abstracted bold M that reads simultaneously as the letter "M" (for "Mes") and as an ascending peak/summit form. Two sharp peaks connected by a central valley create the M shape, while the overall upward-pointing silhouette embodies the Hero's ascent — the act of rising above financial fog into clarity and control.

#### Design Rationale
- **Why M?** — Direct connection to "Mes" (My). Ownership is the brand promise.
- **Why peaks?** — The summit represents achievement, mastery, the Hero's destination. Each peak is a goal reached.
- **Why sharp geometry?** — Precision, decisiveness, control. No soft curves — this is bold energy.
- **Why the open center?** — The negative space between the peaks creates an upward-flowing channel, suggesting the path forward.

#### Symbol Specifications
- ViewBox: 48 x 48
- Minimum size: 16px (favicon)
- Clear space: 1x symbol height on all sides
- The two peaks are equal height, maintaining balance and trustworthiness
- Thick strokes (9 unit leg width) ensure readability at small sizes

## Messaging

**"Prends les devants."**

Direct imperative. Hero archetype energy. Says: you're not reacting to your finances — you're LEADING them. Forward motion. Confidence. The use of "tu" is deliberate: personal, direct, empowering.

### Value Proposition
Mes Finances te donne le controle de ton budget en une configuration. Pas de connexion bancaire, pas de jargon — juste la clarte, chaque mois, en 10 secondes.

### Key Messages
1. **Controle immediat** — "Ton mois est sous controle en un coup d'oeil."
2. **Configuration unique** — "Configure une fois. L'app travaille chaque mois."
3. **Confidentialite totale** — "Tes donnees restent chez toi. Zero connexion bancaire."
4. **Simplicite radicale** — "Une question par ecran. Zero effort."

## What This Direction Is NOT

- **Not aggressive** — Motivating, not pressuring. The tone is "you've got this," not "you're behind."
- **Not masculine** — Energy and confidence are genderless. The warmth of the coral palette and the personal tone ensure it feels inviting to everyone.
- **Not corporate** — Zero jargon, zero formality. This is a personal tool that speaks like a smart friend.
- **Not cold** — The warm coral, the "tu" voice, the encouraging tone — all keep humanity front and center.
